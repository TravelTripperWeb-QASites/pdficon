Know Before You Go To Our Hotel in Hell's Kitchen

Welcome to the metropolis. New York City brings much to the table. A variety of cuisines, languages and culture in the city’s five distinct boroughs. The Washington Jefferson Hotel is conveniently situated in the heart of Midtown with easy access to it all. For your convenience, see the following information about New York before your stay at our hotel in Hell's Kitchen.

Accessibility:

New York City is easy to access via three major airports. LaGuardia Airport (LGA), John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK) and Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) are all within driving distance of our Midtown Manhattan hotel. View driving directions to our hotel and an NYC travel map.

NYC Transportation

Getting Around Midtown NYC

When you stay at the Washington Jefferson Hotel in Midtown NYC, you are in the perfect place to travel like a local New Yorker. Located conveniently near the subway, finding transportation in New York City near our Central Park hotel is a breeze. Simply step out of our conveniently located hotel and hail a taxi to take you anywhere in the city. Use our walking distance guide to explore some of the most iconic landmarks in the city.

Subway

We are just around the corner from the 50th Street Station, where you can catch the A, C, or E line. You can also catch the 1 or 2 trains at 50th and Broadway.Simply purchase a MetroCard from any subway station to enjoy quick, convenient NYC transportation.

Citi Bike

Bicycle rental in NYC is available all over Manhattan via CitiBike. Run by NYC Bike Share, Citi Bikes are available 24/7, 365 days a year, and each station has a touchscreen kiosk, a map of the service area and surrounding neighborhood, and a docking system that releases bikes for rental with a card or key. There are numerous CitiBike stations within moments of our hotel, including 8 Ave & W 52 St, Broadway & W 51 St, and Broadway & W 53 St.

Taxi & Car Service

NYC taxi service and private car service are available to all of our guests. Simply contact our concierge at 1-888-567-7550 to make arrangements.

Airport Transportation

New York City is easy to access via three major airports. LaGuardia Airport (LGA), John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK) and Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) are all within driving distance of the Washington Jefferson Hotel. View driving directions to our hotel and an NYC travel map. We provide convenient NYC airport transportation options to and from our Times Square hotel.

Feel free to contact us for more NYC transportation info to make your stay as efficient and enjoyable as possible.

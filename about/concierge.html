Just Ask Our Manhattan Concierge

Insider Recommendations, Reservations and More

The Washington Jefferson Hotel is delighted to provide friendly Manhattan concierge services for our treasured guests. From restaurant recommendations to transportation assistance, our knowledgeable staff will ensure that your stay in the city is never daunting and always rewarding! Allow us to make reservations for you and share secret tips. Browse some personal recommendations below from our NYC concierge.

Transportation

Our onsite concierge can help arrange private car service to all area airports from our hotel or nearby businesses for easy corporate travel. Or maybe you prefer a limo? We can help with that, too. Can’t quite figure out the NYC transit system? Ask us and we’ll be happy to be of assistance.

Broadway Tickets/Entertainment

Booking tickets for Broadway theater, sports and entertainment events is easy at the Washington Jefferson Hotel. Even if you are searching for a same-day event, our Manhattan hotel concierge can pull some strings and try to get you the best seat in the house. Our hotel near The Gershwin Theater is just a short walk from dozens of classic theaters.

Sightseeing

Whether you would like to book a guided tour or walk the city streets and discover NYC for yourself, our concierge is pleased to provide you with helpful route assistance or insider tips on what to see and where. Visit some of the city’s best museums and explore each unique neighborhood with our help.

If you have any individual needs or requests, please feel free to contact us. Please call us toll free at 1-888-567-7550.

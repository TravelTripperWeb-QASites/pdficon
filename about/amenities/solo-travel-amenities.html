NYC Solo Travel Triumphs at WJ Hotel

Enjoy The Big City In Our Petite Rooms

Ideal for New York City Solo travel, our petite guestroom offers a quaint setting with an abundance of amenities and desirable location. Wake up in an elegant room featuring Frette linens before rebooting with a work out in our fitness center. Then, freshen up with an exhilarating shower and an endless supply of invigorating C.O. Bigelow products. Have a refreshing stay at our Midtown East NYC hotel.

Petite Guestroom Amenities Include:

  • Full length mirror
  • Hairdryer
  • Iron and ironing board
  • Complimentary magazine
  • In-room safe
  • iPod docking station
  • Flat-screen HDTV
  • Free standard Wi-Fi

Whether you’re a solo female traveler on a business trip or visiting Manhattan for pleasure, step out into all the NYC action with peace of mind and the decadent feeling of relaxation, courtesy of the Washington Jefferson Hotel. Whether you’re traveling to New York for the first time or are looking to wander through the best that the city has to offer, we’re happy to offer a safe and accommodating home base for solo travelers. As a chosen destination among solo traveling guests with an adventurous spirit and business travelers alike, our solo travel accommodations are intimate, affordable and within easy access to the city’s most enchanting sights. Nestled in a safe, central location, the Washington Jefferson Hotel is happy to guide you to a number of nearby attractions to make sure that you are as comfortable as possible. Whether you need help navigating the subway, finding tickets for a show or are looking for fine international cuisine, our staff is happy to help.

P.S. Have your solo plans changed? Do you want to bring a friend? The WJ also offers accommodations with two twin beds (at an additional charge). Take on the town together with a girl’s getaway you won’t soon forget.

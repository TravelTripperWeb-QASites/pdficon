Complimentary NYC Walking Tours

Courtesy of Triumph Hotels in Partnership with Streetwise New York

Join us every Thursday at 9:30 am as Washington Jefferson Hotel offers complimentary NYC Walking tours to guests staying in our convenient Theatre District accommodations. Walk along some of the most famous streets in the world with Streetwise Times Square tour. Experience the thrill of Broadway and discover the hidden gems of Hell’s Kitchen, such as world-class restaurants and off-Broadway theaters. As a guest of Washington Jefferson Hotel, you're in the best hotel Hell's Kitchen has to offer, and you'll also have access to the complimentary NYC walking tours available at the following Triumph Hotels:

All tours begin in front of the following locations:

Tuesday- 10:00 a.m.

The Edison Hotel- Times Square
Located in the heart of New York's exciting, historic theater district, explore the excitement of Times Square.

Wednesday- 11:00 a.m.

Hotel Belleclaire - Upper West Side
Take a stroll through the best of the Upper West Side and Central Park, including Strawberry Fields and Bethesda Terrace.

Thursday- 9:30 a.m.

Washington Jefferson Hotel - Hell's Kitchen
Discover the hidden gems of Times Square, its Theater District and the neighboring Hell's Kitchen.

Friday- 9:30 a.m.

Cosmopolitan Hotel - TriBeCa
Explore the art and fashion near the Financial District and new Freedom Tower.

Saturday- 10:00 a.m.

The Evelyn - NoMad
Hotel Chandler - Flatiron
Shop the Italian market and outdoor Union Square Greenmarket in the Flatiron/NoMad District.

Sunday- 10:30 a.m.

Iroquois New York - Midtown
Experience classic Midtown Manhattan at Bryant Park, Grand Central Station, and the New York Public Library.

Request a Reservation

All reservation requests are accepted two weeks in advance to arrival and are based on availability. For more information, please contact the concierge or front desk at your hotel.

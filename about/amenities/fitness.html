Visit Our Midtown Fitness Center

Stay Active At Our Boutique Hotel in Midtown Manhattan

If traversing the busy streets of Manhattan isn’t enough cardio for you, head to our onsite fitness center to get your heart rate going and break a sweat in style. With your room key, you have instant 24-hour access to our fitness center on the first floor. The room is equipped with a TV, towels and the following machines:

  • Tuff Stuff Apollo Series duel weight system: leg, arm, and chest stations for strength training
  • True 700 Treadmill
  • Free weights

Stay active during your stay at our boutique hotel in Midtown Manhattan.

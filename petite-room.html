Planning New York Solo Travel?

Book the Petite Room at the WJ Hotel

For solo travelers, the Petite Room offers an economical choice without sacrificing in-room luxuries. Intimate and contemporary, this affordable Theater District hotel room is simple but sophisticated, outfitted with every comfort you expect to enjoy your time in the city. Rest and recharge in a plush twin bed, tweet your heart out with free Wi-Fi access, and relax in front of a flat-screen HDTV during your New York solo travel adventure.

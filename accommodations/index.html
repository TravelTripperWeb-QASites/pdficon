Contemporary Times Square Accommodations

For the Discerning Times Square Hotel Guest

Sleep in style, even in the midst of the hustle and bustle of Times Square.

The Washington Jefferson Hotel’s Times Square accommodations feature contemporary furnishings and plush amenities. Diverse room selection makes your stay a customizable one, whether your travels bring you on business, leisure, a family outing, or a romantic getaway. From the intimate Petite Room to the Deluxe Rooms and Junior Suite, our Theater District NYC hotel rooms offer the comfort and convenience our discerning guests expect. Amenities available in our accommodations include free standard Wi-Fi access, cable TV, flat-screen HDTVs, working desks and even spa tubs in Superior rooms. Review a full list of amenities to get a better idea of what to expect from our Times Square accommodations and hotel.

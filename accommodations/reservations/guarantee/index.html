Best Rate Guarantee

Get the Best Rate on NYC Hotel Rates

Our best available rate can be found in one place online – www.wjhotel.com. As part of our mission to make every stay special, we are committed to the promise that when you book online through our website, you will receive the best possible rate.

While we maintain strict control over our NYC hotel rates by offering the best prices and specials at www.wjhotel.com, if you find a lower rate on another website within 24 hours of making your reservation, we will honor the lower rate for the total reservation value of the day the booking was made.

How to Claim the Washington Jefferson Hotel Best Rate Guarantee

  • Contact the Washington Jefferson Hotel within 24 hours of making reservations by completing the claim form below
  • Upon verification, we will honor that rate for the nights for which the lower rate was found
  • Rooms on the other website must be publicly available and bookable online at the time of verification

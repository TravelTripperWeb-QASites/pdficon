Enjoy Your Stay At Our Hotel Near The Javits Center

New York City's Premier Convention Center

Located on Eleventh Avenue between 34th and 40th streets, the Jacob K. Javits Convention Center is one of the busiest convention centers in the United States. Designed by architect James Ingo Freed, the convention center was completed in 1986 after six years of construction. Operated by the New York City Convention Center Operating Corporation, the Javits Center has 840,000 square feet of flexible exhibit space, and is New York City’s premier convention facility, hosting prestigious events such as the New York International Auto Show and New York Comic Con.

With a stated mission as the Marketplace of the World, the Javits Center can accommodate events of any size and is the preferred venue for 35,000 companies. Nestled on Manhattan’s bustling West Side, the Javits Center is a central hub of New York City commerce and the core of one of the city’s most resurgent neighborhoods.

Whether hosting or attending an event, guests will find our hotel near the Javits Center a convenient and comfortable choice during their stay. With an ideal location in the heart of Manhattan, the Washington Jefferson Hotel offers modern amenities in a boutique setting perfect for business and leisure travelers alike.

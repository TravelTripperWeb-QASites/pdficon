Avenue Q The Musical

Exclusive Discounted Tickets From Our Theater District Hotel

Washington Jefferson Hotel is proud to partner with the musical Avenue Q, playing at New World Stages, around the corner on West 50th Street. Guests of our Theater District hotel have the opportunity to see the show with 35% off tickets.  

Part flesh, part felt and packed with heart, Avenue Q is a laugh-out-loud musical about a bright-eyed college grad named Princeton, who arrives in the city with big dreams and a tiny bank account. He has to move into a shabby apartment all the way out on Avenue Q. There, he meets Kate, Lucy, Rod, Trekkie, superintendent Gary Coleman (yes, that Gary Coleman), and other new friends. Together, they struggle to find jobs, dates, and their ever-elusive purpose in life.

Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade

Stay Just a Few Blocks from the Parade

There are few family-friendly activities as exciting as the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. Guests staying at our Theater District hotel for Thanksgiving will have a convenient, two-block walk to the spectacular event. 

The NYC parade is celebrating its 90th anniversary by once again taking over Manhattan. The festivities begin at 9am, starting at 77th Street and Central Park West. Most of the route is along 6th Avenue from 59th Street all the way to the world famous Macy’s on 34th Street.

Staying at our hotel near the Thanksgiving Day Parade is the best way to make sure you beat the crowd that morning. Make sure you and your family get the chance to see amazing parade attractions like Pikachu, Spongebob, and the big guy himself, Santa Claus.

Guests will also be treated to our premium accommodations. Choose from a collection of rooms and suites, all featuring modern furniture, Wi-Fi, and HDTVs.

Book your stay at Washington Jefferson now and make unforgettable memories at the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade.

Complimentary Holiday Walking Tours

Courtesy of Triumph Hotels in Partnership With NYC Touring Company, Streetwise.

It's the most wonderful time of the year—and by "it" we mean Christmas in New York! Just minutes after The Macy’s Thanksgiving Parade, the city and the best New York attractions turn into an illuminated winter wonderland, decked out with Christmas trees, holiday windows and Christmas lights. Get the most out of the merriest season with our guided Holiday Walking Tours courtesy of Triumph Hotels. Streetwise, NYC’s premier in the know tour guides, will take you on a holiday themed tour of New York City’s Theater District, Washington Jefferson Hotel’s lively neighborhood.

Every Thursday from December 1st through December 29th at 9:30 a.m., Washington Jefferson Hotel presents complimentary Holiday NYC Walking tours to guests staying in our contemporary accommodations. Experience the classic holiday hustle and bustle of NYC with a Streetwise Theater District walking tour. Take in the spectacular holiday windows, explore dining in Hell’s Kitchen, and walk the same streets that many of Broadway’s most notable actors and actresses have walked. As a guest of the Washington Jefferson Hotel, you can also access the additional complimentary NYC walking tours available at the following Triumph Hotels.

All tours begin in front of the following locations:

November 29- December 27, Every Tuesday-10:00 a.m.
Edison Hotel - Times Square Tour
Located in the heart of New York's exciting, historic theater district, explore the excitement the Holidays in Times Square.

November 30- December 28, Every Wednesday-11:00 a.m.
Hotel Belleclaire - Upper West Side
Take a stroll through the best of the Upper West Side and enjoy the holidays in one of New York’s most beautiful neighborhoods.

December 1- December 29, Every Thursday-9:30 a.m.
Washington Jefferson Hotel - Hell's Kitchen
Discover the hidden gems of Holidays in Times Square, the Theater District and neighboring Hell's Kitchen.

November 25- December 30, Every Friday-9:30 a.m.
Cosmopolitan Hotel - TriBeCa
Explore the Holiday splendor near the Financial District and new Freedom Tower.

November 26- December 24, Every Saturday-10:00 a.m.
The Evelyn - NoMad
Hotel Chandler - Flatiron
Enjoy the culinary treats of the Holiday season, Shop the Italian market and outdoor Union Square Greenmarket in the Flatiron/NoMad District.

November 27- December 31, Every Sunday-10:30 a.m.
Iroquois New York - Midtown
Experience classic Midtown Manhattan and the Holiday windows of 5th Avenue and the wonder of Bryant Park.

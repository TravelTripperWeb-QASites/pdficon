The WJ Hotel Near Terminal 5 NYC

Acclaimed Acts Mere Minutes From Your Room

New York City is one of the finest cities in the world for music. Home to aspiring and renowned musicians of all genres, the city also boasts some of the country’s best music venues. Among the many great places to see a live show, Terminal 5 in Hell’s Kitchen has increasingly become known for hosting a wide array of talented acts since it opened in 2003.

Located on 56th Street just west of Eleventh Avenue, Terminal 5 is a multi-level event space in Manhattan. With five distinct rooms and a total capacity of 3,000 people, Terminal 5 is ideal for shows of varying sizes. Hosting everyone from local DJs, regional bands, and up-and-coming singers to critically acclaimed performers like Kendrick Lamar, Mac Miller, Sleater-Kinney, MUTEMATH, and more, Terminal 5 continuously draws an eclectic and impressive lineup of shows.

An Ideal Choice for NYC Solo Travel

Stay in a Safe, Central Urban Refuge

Whether you are planning NYC solo travel because you have an adventurous spirit, or because business is calling you—or both—the Washington Jefferson Hotel is the smart choice for your unique needs. Our Petite Room is ideal for anyone traveling alone, road-trippers making their way across the country, corporate guests and visitors from other countries. This accommodation is intimate, cozy, incredibly affordable, and within easy access of all the excitement of the city beyond our doors.

Located in a safe, central location, the Washington Jefferson Hotel is especially suitable for solo travel to New York City. Whether this is your first time in the city, or you’re visiting again, we have the knowledge and expertise to guide you along the way. Because we are a boutique hotel, this means we can spend more time catering to your needs and seeing that you are as comfortable as possible. If you need help navigating the subway, or maybe finding tickets for a show, we are happy to assist.

You may be traveling alone, but you’re not alone at the WJ Hotel. You don’t need to feel small in the big city. Start planning solo travel to NYC by reading more about our Petite Room. We look forward to having you.
